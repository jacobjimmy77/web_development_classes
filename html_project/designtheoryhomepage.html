What is design theory?

Design theory is a research field concerned with the theoretical approaches that help artist understand basic design principles and best practices. Graphic designers often apply this theories when creating images for marketing purposes, such as market campaigns or brand management. It helps artist understand how to effectively convey a massage to an audience through visual cues.
